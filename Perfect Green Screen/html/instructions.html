Perfect Green Screen

Main Screen
  1. Bands Slider - Select the number of band syou would like to see for the green screen.
      A good starting point is around 9-10 bands. The objective is to fit as much of the green into one or two bands for simple keying. If you see lots of gradation in the bands (lots of banding) - that is bad, you need to work on making a better key.
  2. Two Views are available:
    • Both Views - Shows actual Camera View on the left split screen and the Bands View on the right Screen
    • Band View - Only shows Bands View and removes the Camera View.
  3. Exposure - You can lock the exposure
  4. Focus - You can lock the focus
  5. Info - Info takes you to this page
  6. Settings - Gives you some Options on a Settings Screen
Settings Screen
  1. Histogram - Turn histogram on or off
  2. Max Bands - you can change the max bands from 10 to 50. But more than 20 is really not that useful unless you are aching for some pain and serious work to try and make it truely a perfect green screen!
  3. Email Support: If you have a question, spot a bug or have a feature you want to request - email us!
